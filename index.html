    Pointer

    When your mouse pointer hovers over a clickable item, it will turn into a hand cursor and you can click on that item. Clicking on the Icon will take you to Home Page.

    Clickable Objects

    When using mobile devices your cursor will not change into a pointer. Look out for items that are glowing. You can click items on the screen that have a glow behind them. Clicking on these items will give you more information.

    Timeline

    You can navigate the screens by clicking on the timeline or the small dots at the bottom of the screen. Clicking on a dot takes you to its respective screen.

    Audio

    Clicking on the audio icon will play an audio sound.
